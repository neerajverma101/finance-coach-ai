# üöÄ Personal Finance Coach - Product Overview

A simple, trustworthy personal finance app that helps people understand their money, fix problems, and build wealth step by step.

---

## üíî The Problem

Most people struggle with money because:
- **They don't know where they stand** - No clear picture of their financial health
- **They follow random advice** - Generic tips that don't fit their situation
- **They feel overwhelmed** - Too much jargon, too many options
- **They give up quickly** - No clear path or tracking system
- **They can't stay consistent** - No accountability or nudges

**Existing solutions are broken:**
- Banks show accounts, not insights
- Advisors are expensive and pushy  
- Budgeting apps are too manual
- Investment platforms assume knowledge
- None of them actually *teach* or *coach*

---

## ‚ú® Our Solution

A **personal finance coach in your pocket** that:

1. **Understands You**
   - Takes your full financial picture (income, expenses, assets, debts, goals)
   - Learns your risk tolerance and knowledge level
   - Adapts to your life stage and priorities

2. **Finds Problems**
   - Calculates net worth, debt health, savings rate
   - Identifies what's working and what's broken
   - Prioritizes issues (emergency fund gaps, high-interest debt, poor allocation)

3. **Organizes Your Money**
   - Clear buckets: Emergency, Debt, Short-term Goals, Long-term Wealth
   - Each bucket shows current status, target, and next action
   - Visual, simple, no spreadsheets

4. **Gives You a Plan**
   - Top 3 actions to take (not 50)
   - Clear monthly targets (save X, invest Y, pay Z)
   - Timeline to achieve each goal

5. **Tracks Progress**
   - Monthly self-reported check-ins (no bank access needed)
   - System compares actual vs target
   - Detects if you're on track, ahead, or behind

6. **Keeps You Going**
   - Weekly encouragement messages
   - Corrections when you slip
   - Celebrations when you hit milestones
   - "Why this?" explanations when you're confused

## üß† Our Secret Sauce: Smart Use of AI

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **We DON'T use AI for everything - only where it helps**

### ‚ùå AI Does NOT
- Calculate your money (we use deterministic math)
- Make investment decisions (rule-based logic)
- Handle your data (encrypted, auditable)

### ‚úÖ AI DOES
- **Explain** recommendations in simple language
- **Answer** your finance questions using verified sources
- **Orchestrate** complex workflows (parsing statements, running simulations)
- **Personalize** language based on your knowledge level

**Why this matters:**
- **Trustworthy** - Every number is traceable, auditable
- **Explainable** - You always know "why"
- **Compliant** - No black-box decisions
- **Affordable** - AI only where needed, not everywhere
